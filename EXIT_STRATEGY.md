# PayGuard: The 30-Day Acquisition Playbook

This is the plan to move PayGuard from "side project" to an "acquired feature" within 30 days.

## 1. The Asset (What we have)
- **Tech:** Visual-layer ML firewall. Scans rendered pixels for scams (TLDs, patterns, AI images, deepfakes).
- **Unique Edge:** Traditional tools check URLs/reputation. PayGuard checks the *truth*—the rendered screen.
- **Performance:** Sub-second (0.5s) scan cycle. Parallel processing (OCR, Vision, ML).

## 2. Immediate Distribution (Week 1)
- **Landing Page:** Professional React-based landing page (built at `frontend/src/App.js`).
- **Waitlist:** Connect a simple email capture (e.g., PostHog/Mailchimp/Typeform).
- **App Store:** Finalize the binary for the macOS App Store (Utilities category). This provides instant credibility to buyers.

## 3. Targeting The Strategic Buyers (Week 2)
Focus on companies where PayGuard solves a high-cost fraud or churn problem.
